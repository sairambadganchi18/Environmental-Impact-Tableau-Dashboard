# 🌍 Environmental Impact Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Environmental Impact Tableau Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project designed to analyze and visualize environmental sustainability metrics across different industries and regions. The dashboard helps organizations monitor carbon emissions, energy consumption, water usage, recycling performance, and compliance levels to support data-driven environmental decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze environmental performance across industries.
- Monitor carbon emissions and resource consumption.
- Compare sustainability metrics across different regions.
- Track recycling rates and compliance levels.
- Support decision-making through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Carbon Emissions
- Average Recycling Rate
- Total Energy Usage
- Total Water Consumption

### Interactive Filters
- Region
- Industry
- Product Type
- Compliance Level
- Year

### Visualizations
- Bar Chart – Carbon Emissions by Region
- Line Chart – Energy Usage Trend
- Heatmap – Recycling Rate by Industry and Region
- Pie Chart – Compliance Level Distribution
- Scatter Plot – Carbon Emissions vs Recycling Rate

## 📁 Dataset Information

The dataset contains environmental sustainability data including:

- Carbon Emissions (kg)
- Water Consumption (liters)
- Energy Usage (kWh)
- Waste Generated (kg)
- Recycling Rate (%)
- Air Quality Index (AQI)
- Region
- Industry
- Product Type
- Category
- Subcategory
- Compliance Level
